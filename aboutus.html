About Us

New Hampshire's Premier High School Basketball Coverage

Our Mission

Ball603 is dedicated to providing comprehensive coverage of New Hampshire high school basketball. We believe every player, every team, and every game deserves recognition. Our goal is to be the definitive source for Granite State hoops.

What We Cover

  • Game Coverage – Scores, recaps, and box scores from games across all divisions
  • Photo Galleries – Action shots from photographers throughout the state
  • Video – Highlights, features, and game broadcasts
  • Features – In-depth stories, player profiles, and Mike Whaley's Jam Session column
  • Historical Archives – NHIAA championship history and the 1K in the 603 scorers database

Our Team

Ball603 is powered by a network of dedicated photographers, videographers, and contributors who share a passion for New Hampshire basketball.

Get Involved

Interested in contributing to Ball603? We're always looking for photographers, videographers, and writers to help cover games across the state. Contact us to learn more about joining our team.
